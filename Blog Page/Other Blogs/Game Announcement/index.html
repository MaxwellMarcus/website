My First Game

        I decided that the first project that I am going to blog about is the creation of a game. Over the next couple of weeks I will take you with me on my journey of creating a game. My plan for the game is to make a dungeon crawler, where the player will have to explore a dungeon to get loot, so they can buy weapons, and, eventually, take down a final boss. Hopefully, I will be able to upload a blog almost everyday where I will show everybody the progress I have made.

        This is just a really quick announcement blog, but starting tomorrow more blogs, with much more detail, will be uploaded. I hope that you are excited as I am to be making a game. Have a wonderful day!
